üìö Metric Explanations

Accuracy

Percentage of correct predictions among all predictions. Formula: (TP + TN) / (TP + TN + FP + FN)

Precision

Of the predicted positive cases, what percentage was actually positive. Formula: TP / (TP + FP)

Recall (Sensitivity)

Of the actual positive cases, what percentage did the model catch. Formula: TP / (TP + FN)

F1-Score

Harmonic mean of precision and recall. Better when there's class imbalance. Formula: 2 √ó (Precision √ó Recall) / (Precision + Recall)
